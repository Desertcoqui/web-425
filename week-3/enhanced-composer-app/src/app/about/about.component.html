Robi Draco



Draco Cornelius Rosa Suarez also known as Robi Draco Rosa is a Puerto Rican musician and composer. He started his career in 1980 under the group Menudo. His early career friends in the group included now famous Ricky Martin. He has composed six albums for Ricky Martin, Julio Iglesias and himself. He has won a total of five Grammy Awards. Little known fact: He received song writing credit because of the interpolation of part of the composition for Sisqo’s “Thong Song” released February 15, 2000.
