Munro Leaf

The author Munroe Leaf is an American writer of children books. During his impressive 40 year career he has written over 40 books before passing away in 1976 at the age of 71. His most famous book was Ferdinand the Bull which he wrote in less the one hour on a yellow legal-length pad. The story follows a gentle bull in rural Spain who prefers smelling flowers to bullfighting. The book has been translated to over 60 foreign languages.
